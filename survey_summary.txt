WHO RESPONDED:
  • Hard of Hearing: 4 respondents

  • Deaf: 4 respondents
  • APD: 3 respondents
  • Lipreading learner: 3 respondents
  • ADHD with auditory difficulties: 1 respondents

KEY FINDINGS:
  • 17/20 said animated mouth shapes would be useful or potentially useful
  • 17/20 already use lipreading or visual cues naturally
  • 12/20 always use closed captions
  • Average caption helpfulness rating: 4.1 / 5.0
  • Most requested platform: In-person learning

CONCLUSION:
  85% of respondents validated the core concept of LipSync Assistant.
  The data suggests strong demand for visual pronunciation support,
  particularly on In-person learning and in-person learning environments.
